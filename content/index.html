I'm a senior oceanographer at Fugro GEOS Ltd where I am responsible for the development and deployment of numerical models for operational weather forecasting and the production of hindcast models for the company's consultancy group.

Previously I was a post-doctoral researcher at the University of Oxford, where I did research and taught in the sub-department of Atmospheric, Oceanic & Planetary Physics.
